Securely split and combine secrets with QR codes

What is QR SafeShare?

QR SafeShare is a tool to split a password, recovery phrase, or any text into multiple QR codes using mathematical techniques (XOR and Shamir‚Äôs Secret Sharing). A single share has no value; only by combining the right shares is the original secret reconstructed. Everything happens fully locally in your browser ‚Äì nothing is stored online.

How does splitting work?

You enter your secret (for example a password or recovery phrase). The tool uses mathematical calculations to create multiple shares. Each share is shown as a QR code and can be stored or shared separately.

How does combining work?

Use your camera or upload to scan the QR codes. As soon as enough shares are present, the original secret is reconstructed automatically. Reconstruction happens fully locally in the browser.

3D-printable QR codes

With QR SafeShare you can not only store your secret QR codes digitally, but also export them as a 3MF file. This format is directly supported by most 3D printers and lets you create a durable, physical backup of your secret.

Why is this useful? Paper can tear, fade, or burn. Digital storage can be hacked, corrupted, or lost. A 3D-printed QR is durable, tangible, and completely offline ‚Äì a secure backup you can actually hold in your hands.

Tip: in your slicer, add a color change just above 2 mm. Print the base in white and the top in black (or another dark color) to ensure the QR code remains scannable.

Protecting your 3D-printed QR codes

Want to make sure no one can simply scan your 3D-printed QR code? Use the dedicated QR SafeShare Sleeve. Slide the QR tile inside and secure it with a padlock through the hole, so it cannot be removed or scanned without your permission.

For the technically inclined

QR SafeShare supports two mathematical methods:

  • XOR: the secret is combined with random data. A single share contains no information. Two shares are required to get the secret back.
  • Shamir‚Äôs Secret Sharing: a well-known cryptographic method where the secret is embedded in a polynomial. You can configure how many shares are required to reconstruct the secret.

Both methods are mathematically secure as long as you follow the rules. Everything runs client-side in your browser with JavaScript.

Frequently asked questions

What is a recovery phrase?

A recovery phrase is a series of words used to access a crypto wallet. QR SafeShare can safely split and restore such phrases.

Can I use any text?

Yes, besides passwords and recovery phrases you can also enter any text and store it in QR codes.

Is this just splitting, or is it also secure?

The secret is not simply cut into pieces. QR SafeShare uses techniques like XOR and Shamir‚Äôs Secret Sharing. A single share is useless; only by combining enough shares the secret is restored.

Why are QRs automatically scaled larger?

If the payload contains a lot of data, the website automatically scales the QR for reliable scanning.

Does everything run locally?

Yes, all computations run in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or shared with a server.
